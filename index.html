Welcome to DevDesk Queue

We are a platform created to help Lambda students with all their concerns that come up when learning.

DevDesk Queue is a simple platform to get that extra hand while you are coding.

What we do?

We help students get the help they need by interacting with staff by just creating a ticket.

We focus on supporting and improving the experience of getting help while on Lambda.

It allows students to submit a help desk ticket, categorize it and post it to the help channel.

How it works?

This app allows an admin to manage help desk tickets that come in from Lambda School Students.

As a student you want to log in and have the ability to see tickets that are currently open for help.

As a student you want to be able to create a new help ticket with a title, description, what you've tried and a category (i.e. React).
